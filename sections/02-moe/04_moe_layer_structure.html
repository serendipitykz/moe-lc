MoE 层结构详解

每层参数量

稠密：\(2 \times d_{\text{model}} \times d_{ff}\)   |   MoE：\(N_{\text{exp}} \times (2 \times d_{\text{model}} \times d_{ff}) + \text{gating}\)

每 token 激活

稠密：\(2 \times d_{\text{model}} \times d_{ff}\) FLOPs   |   MoE：\(k \times (2 \times d_{\text{model}} \times d_{ff})\) FLOPs
